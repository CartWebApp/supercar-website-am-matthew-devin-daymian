    HISTORY

    Creating your future

    Trion Supercars, a company known for its high-performance hypercars, has its roots in the automotive landscape of 2013. Founded by David S. Brown, a visionary entrepreneur with a passion for pushing the boundaries of automotive engineering, Trion Supercars emerged with the ambitious goal of creating a new breed of hypercar that would redefine luxury and performance. Their first creation, the Nemesis, was unveiled in 2016, showcasing an innovative design and a powerful engine that promised to deliver exceptional speed and handling. Since then, Trion has continued to refine its technology and expand its offerings, culminating in the development of the Nemesis RR, a fully electric hypercar that pushes the limits of performance and sustainability. Today, Trion stands as a testament to the relentless pursuit of innovation and excellence in the world of high-performance automobiles.
